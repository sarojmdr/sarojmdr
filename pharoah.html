Pharoh : Ramses The Great

1309B.C - 1213B.C

One Of The Greatest Pharoh Ever Lived On Earth

Ramesses II, also known as Ramesses the Great, was the third pharaoh of the Nineteenth Dynasty of Egypt.
He is often regarded as the greatest, most celebrated, and most powerful pharaoh of the New Kingdom,
itself the most powerful period of Ancient Egypt.
